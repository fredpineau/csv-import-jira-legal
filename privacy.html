Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-18

This Privacy Policy describes how the application CSV Import for Jira Select Fields (“the App”) processes information when used with Atlassian Jira Cloud.

1. Data processing

The App processes only the data necessary to perform its functionality. This may include custom field identifiers, context identifiers, custom field option values, and CSV content uploaded by the user.

2. Data storage

The App does not store customer data outside Atlassian infrastructure. Uploaded CSV content is processed only to perform the requested action.

3. Data sharing

The App does not share customer data with third parties.

4. Security

The App is built on the Atlassian Forge platform and uses Atlassian-managed authentication and permissions.

5. Data retention

The App does not intentionally retain uploaded CSV data after processing.
